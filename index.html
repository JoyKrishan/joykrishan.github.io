Hi, there!

Currently, I am affiliated with the Software Research Lab at the University of Saskatchewan, where I am immersed in cutting-edge research spanning automated program repair, software mining, and the utilization of large language models in software engineering. This journey is part of my thesis-based master's program, guided by the esteemed Prof. Chanchal Roy. My academic journey began at BRAC University, where I graduated with Highest Distinction in 2020. During my time there, my passion for research blossomed, particularly in the realms of machine learning and signal processing. Under the guidance of Prof. Md. Jalil Piran and Prof. Amitabha Chakraborty, I delved into various projects, laying the groundwork for my current pursuits. Additionally, I had the privilege of contributing as a volunteer teacher for Stanford University's Code in Place program and served as a mentor for the Natural Language Processing Specialization offered by DeepLearning.AI, further enriching my understanding and passion for the field.
